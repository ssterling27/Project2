Moodify

Ever wish you could hear all of your favorite music in one place without the ones you don't want to hear? Or just listen to certain songs depending on what you're feeling in that moment? This app can create customizable playlists according to your tastes without those unwanted songs. Connect with fellow music lovers across the world on you love or dislike of songs.
